- **Quarterly:** revenue growth, GMV, Subscription/Merchant split, gross margin, opex/revenue, FCF margin, SBC, GPV penetration, guidance.
- **Semiannual:** enterprise wins, Plus share, B2B disclosures, international mix, POS momentum.
- **Event-driven:** AI/agentic commerce product updates, Google/OpenAI/Microsoft integrations, antitrust/regulatory headlines, large buyback activity.
- **Valuation trigger:** revisit recommendation if stock < $90, > $130, or if consensus FY2026/FY2027 revenue changes by >5%.
