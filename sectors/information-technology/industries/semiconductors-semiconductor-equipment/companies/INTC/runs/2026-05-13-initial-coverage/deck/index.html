Risk Register

Key Risks — Probability & Severity Matrix

R1 — 18A Node Failure CRITICAL
Probability: ~30% | Impact: −30% to −50% stock price. 10nm delay precedent cost 3 years & $10B+ in share. Repeat would invalidate foundry thesis entirely.
Mitigant: Panther Lake validated basic 18A; AWS $15B+ commitment provides revenue floor.
R2 — AMD/ARM Data Center Share HIGH
Probability: ~40% | Impact: DCAI revenue stalls at $15-17B vs. $30B bull case. AMD EPYC +2pp/yr; ARM-based CPUs now ~25% of data center.
Mitigant: CPU inference tailwind; Xeon 6 competitive on rack density/TCO.
R3 — Foundry Losses Persist HIGH
Probability: ~40% | Q4 2025: −44% op. margin. Breakeven requires $8B+ ext. revenue, multi-customer ramp, and yield learning curve — all uncertain through 2028.
Mitigant: AWS relationship anchors early revenue; CHIPS Act capital reduces cash burn.
R4 — Capital Structure Stress MODERATE
Probability: ~25% | $47B gross debt, negative FCF through FY2026E. Bear case forces equity raise or balance sheet stress by FY2027.
Mitigant: $7.86B CHIPS grants, $11B loan facility available, $24B cash.
R5 — Gaudi AI Irrelevance MODERATE
Probability: ~55% | NVIDIA controls >90% of AI accelerator revenue. Gaudi 3 has achieved minimal market traction despite competitive pricing at hyperscalers.
Mitigant: CPU inference thesis partially offsets; GPU irrelevance is a growth ceiling, not an existential threat.
R6 — China Revenue Risk MODERATE
Probability: ~35% | China ~25% of INTC revenue historically. Expanding U.S. export controls + domestic alternatives (Loongson, Zhaoxin) accelerating. Potential $13B revenue risk.
Mitigant: U.S. government relationship provides some protection; Intel diversifying geographically.
