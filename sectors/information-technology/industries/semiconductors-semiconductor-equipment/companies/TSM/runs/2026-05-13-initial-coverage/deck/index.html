04 · Thesis Tracker — Risk Register

Key Risks — Ranked by Severity × Probability

CRITICAL
Taiwan Strait Conflict / Coercive Action
Tail risk but existential if realized. Mitigant: geographic fab diversification (Arizona, Japan); government intervention incentive.
HIGH
AI CapEx Cycle Reversal
Hyperscaler capex pullback would hit HPC revenue (61% mix). Mitigant: long-lead order commitments, CoWoS backlog.
HIGH
US Export Control Tightening
BIS restrictions on advanced chips to China. 2025 China revenue ~12%. Tariff uncertainty adds near-term volatility.
MEDIUM
N2 Margin Dilution — Yield Ramp
New node ramps historically compress gross margins 2-4 ppt. N2 GAA is more complex than N3 FinFET. Monitored via QoQ gross margin trend.
MEDIUM
Samsung GAA Recovery (SF2)
Samsung targeting 20% foundry share by 2027; Tesla AI deal potential. If SF2 yield improves, could win back marginal customers.
LOW
NTD/USD Currency & FX Headwinds
Revenue USD-denominated but costs NTD-denominated. Appreciation is natural hedge; 10% NTD move = ~3-4 ppt gross margin impact.
