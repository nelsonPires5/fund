Risk Assessment

Key Risks — Ranked by Severity × Probability

CRITICAL

NAND Pricing Reversal

Samsung/SK Hynix capacity additions trigger 30%+ ASP decline. GM could collapse to teens as in 2023. Primary cycle risk vector.

Mitigant: LT contracts; BiCS8 cost floor; GM buffer above cost

HIGH

AI CapEx Concentration

~70–80% of AI NAND demand concentrated in 4 hyperscalers. Any capex pause creates a demand cliff with minimal notice.

Mitigant: Multi-customer diversification; edge AI + 5G secondary vectors

HIGH

Samsung Cap Expansion

Korean NAND producers accelerating bit growth beyond demand. Samsung's discipline is single biggest external variable for SNDK margins.

Mitigant: BiCS8 cost advantage; hyperscaler qualification head start

MED-HIGH

Kioxia JV / Japan FX Risk

100% NAND wafers from Japan JV. Yen appreciation raises manufacturing costs. $1.165B fixed payment obligation 2026–29.

Mitigant: JV locked 2034; Japan CHIPS Act funding; yen hedges

MEDIUM

WDC 19.9% Overhang

WDC stake subject to 2-yr tax restriction expiring Feb 2027. Secondary offering potential could pressure shares and multiple.

Mitigant: WDC incentivized to sell at premium; overhang reducing as date nears
