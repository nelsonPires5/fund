Company Overview

What Is Nebius Group?

Nebius is an AI-native cloud infrastructure company spun out of Yandex N.V. in 2024. It provides full-stack GPU cloud compute, MLOps tooling, managed AI services, and developer AI products including Toloka (data labeling) and TripleTen (AI-skills training). HQ in Amsterdam with data centers in Europe, North America, and expanding globally.

Investment Thesis

6 Pillars of the Bull Case

① AI Cloud TAM Expansion

GPU cloud market ~45% CAGR through 2028. Nebius captures EU & EM sovereignty demand where hyperscalers face regulatory friction.

② Meta Hyperscaler Anchor

$27B 2-year GPU supply agreement with Meta (Feb 2026) provides revenue certainty and de-risks the 2026–27 ramp substantially.

③ Yandex DNA Moat

Team built & operated Russia's largest AI cloud at scale. Deep ML/infra engineering talent creates a defensible tech moat.

④ Margin Inflection Coming

Gross margin 44%→48% (FY2024–25). Leveraged fixed base means EBITDA inflection above $600M revenue.

⑤ Platform Flywheel

GPU IaaS → MLOps → Toloka → TripleTen: closed AI development ecosystem with high switching costs and data moats.

⑥ Geopolitical Tailwind

EU AI Act & data sovereignty regs push enterprises toward European-domiciled cloud providers. Nebius uniquely positioned.
